Registration Guide

How to Register

Ready to join? Here's everything you need to know to get your team registered for the competition.

01

Build Your Team

Get together with 3-5 friends from your school or university. You'll need to pick a team leader who'll be our main point of contact.

02

Work on Your Idea

Think of a cool IoT project that solves an actual problem. Don't worry about making it perfect - we want to see your creativity and thinking process.

03

Grab the Template

We've made a template to help you organize your proposal. Download it from our website - it'll make things way easier!

04

Write Your Proposal

Fill out the template with your project details - what you're building, why it matters, and how you plan to do it. Keep it clear and simple.

05

Fill Out the Form

Head to the registration form and enter your team details. Make sure all the info is correct - we'll use this to contact you!

06

Submit Everything

Upload your proposal and hit submit. Once we get it, you'll get a confirmation email. Then you're all set!
